If you want to publish a paper

Writing

Lessons from reviews:

  1. Topics on irregular sampled clinical and time-series data can be promising.
  2. Some questions people may concerns theoretically: is your estimator unbiased? Low variances? Will your algorithm converges(From Prof Yang’s question and a formula in the paper about compressive sensing and SoV)?
  3. Good and convincing evaluations of the method is vital. That’s what Atlas’s points of not caring too much about the values, but caring the methods of showing and explaining your results, especially there exists some uncommon results.
  4. A clear line. People will read your experiments with your motivation. It’s important to be consistent.
  5. Grammar and sentences.
